<p><p>The Copper Selenium (CuSe) evaporation material market finds applications primarily in electronics manufacturing, particularly in the deposition processes of thin films. In electron beam evaporation, CuSe is vaporized using focused electron beams, ensuring high purity and precision for semiconductor devices. Thermal evaporation utilizes resistance heating to convert CuSe into vapor, suitable for larger-area coatings.
