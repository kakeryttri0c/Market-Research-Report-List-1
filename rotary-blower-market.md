<p><p>The rotary blower market is segmented into low, medium, and high pressure categories based on operational pressure levels. Low pressure rotary blowers are designed for applications like aeration and material handling, operating at pressures typically up to 2 psi. Medium pressure rotary blowers serve industrial processes requiring pressures between 2 to 15 psi, often in pneumatic conveying and wastewater treatment. High pressure rotary blowers operate beyond 15 psi, ideal for applications needing significant pressure, such as vacuum systems and gas boosting.</p></p>
