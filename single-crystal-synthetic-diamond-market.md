<p><strong>The Single Crystal Synthetic Diamond Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HPHT Diamond</li><li>CVD Diamond</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single crystal synthetic diamond market comprises two primary types: HPHT (High Pressure High Temperature) and CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) diamonds. HPHT diamonds are produced by simulating natural diamond-forming conditions, resulting in robust and high-quality stones mainly used in industrial applications. In contrast, CVD diamonds are created through a vaporization process that allows for greater control over purity and size, making them suitable for both industrial uses and gemstone markets. Both segments are rapidly growing due to increasing demand across various industries.</p></p>
