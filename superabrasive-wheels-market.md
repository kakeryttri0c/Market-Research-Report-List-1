Diamond wheels are known for their exceptional hardness, making them ideal for cutting, grinding, and shaping hard materials like ceramics and stone. In contrast, CBN wheels are preferred for grinding ferrous materials due to their superior thermal stability and wear resistance. Both types of wheels are critical in manufacturing and industrial applications, offering enhanced precision, efficiency, and durability in various machining processes.</p></p>
