<p><strong>The Primary Switchgears Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-insulated</li><li>Vacuum-insulated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The primary switchgear market is primarily categorized into gas-insulated switchgear (GIS) and vacuum-insulated switchgear (VIS). GIS employs sulfur hexafluoride gas for insulation and is compact, ideal for urban areas with space constraints. It offers high reliability and minimal maintenance. Conversely, VIS utilizes a vacuum to insulate and interrupt electrical currents, providing robust protection against environmental factors and facilitating efficient operation. Both types are crucial for managing electrical distribution in substations and industrial applications, enhancing safety and performance.</p></p>
