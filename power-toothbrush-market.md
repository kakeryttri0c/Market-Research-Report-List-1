<p><strong>The Power Toothbrush Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vibration</li><li>Rotation-oscillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power toothbrush market is primarily segmented into two types: vibration and rotation-oscillation. Vibration toothbrushes utilize rapid side-to-side movements to effectively clean teeth and gums, providing a gentle yet thorough brushing experience. In contrast, rotation-oscillation toothbrushes feature a round brush head that rotates in one direction and then the opposite, mimicking the motion of a dentist's tool, ensuring comprehensive plaque removal. Both types aim to enhance oral hygiene and provide superior cleaning compared to manual brushing.</p></p>
