<p><strong>The Respiratory Monitoring Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spirometry</li><li>Plethysmography</li><li>IOS/FOT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Respiratory monitoring devices are essential for assessing lung function and diagnosing respiratory conditions. Spirometry measures airflow and volume during breathing, providing critical data for conditions like asthma and COPD. Plethysmography evaluates lung capacity by measuring changes in air pressure within a sealed environment. Impulse Oscillometry System (IOS) and Forced Oscillation Technique (FOT) assess respiratory resistance and reactance during breathing, offering insights into distal airway function. Collectively, these technologies are vital for effective respiratory care and management.</p></p>
