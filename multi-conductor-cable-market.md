<p><ul><li>Shielded</li><li>Unshielded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-conductor cable market is categorized into shielded and unshielded types. Shielded cables have a protective layer that minimizes electromagnetic interference, making them ideal for sensitive applications where signal integrity is crucial. Unshielded cables, on the other hand, lack this protection, making them more cost-effective and suitable for environments with low interference. The choice between shielded and unshielded cables depends on factors like installation conditions, required signal quality, and budget constraints, impacting various industries including telecommunications and automation.</p></p>
