<p><strong>The Metallic Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Stamping Foil</li><li>Cold Stamping Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic foil market comprises two primary types: hot stamping foil and cold stamping foil. Hot stamping foil is applied using heat and pressure, allowing for vibrant, lustrous designs on various surfaces. It's commonly used in packaging, labels, and decorative items. In contrast, cold stamping foil uses adhesive rather than heat, facilitating efficient production and application on a wider range of substrates. Both methods enhance visual appeal, providing a premium finish for products while catering to different manufacturing processes and design needs.</p></p>
