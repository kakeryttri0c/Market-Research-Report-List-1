<p><strong>The Light Automobile Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Power Steering</li><li>Electronic Power Steering</li><li>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light automobile steering systems include three primary types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), which uses hydraulic pressure to assist in steering; Electronic Power Steering (EPS), which employs electric motors for smoother and more efficient steering, often allowing for adjustable feedback; and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), which combines both hydraulic and electric technologies to enhance performance and energy efficiency. Each system offers distinct advantages in terms of weight, fuel efficiency, and driving dynamics, catering to varied vehicle requirements and consumer preferences.</p></p>
