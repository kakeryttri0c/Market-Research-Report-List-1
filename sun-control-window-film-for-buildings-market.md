<p><p>The Sun Control Window Film market for buildings is segmented into three primary types: Clear (Non-Reflective), Dyed (Non-Reflective), and Vacuum Coated (Reflective). Clear films allow natural light while reducing heat and glare without altering aesthetics. Dyed films absorb solar energy, adding color and privacy without reflectivity. Vacuum coated films utilize a reflective metallic layer that effectively minimizes solar heat gain while maintaining visibility. Each type serves distinct architectural needs and preferences, enhancing energy efficiency and occupant comfort.</p></p>
