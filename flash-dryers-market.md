<p><ul><li>Direct Drying</li><li>Indirect Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flash dryers are essential in the drying process for various industries, employing two primary methods: direct and indirect drying. Direct drying involves hot air streaming directly into the material, rapidly evaporating moisture, while indirect drying utilizes heat exchangers or surfaces to transfer thermal energy without direct contact. Each method caters to different materials and drying requirements, impacting energy efficiency and product quality. The choice between direct and indirect drying often depends on the specific application and desired end product characteristics.</p></p>
