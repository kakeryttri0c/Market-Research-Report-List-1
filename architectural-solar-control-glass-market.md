<p><p>Architectural solar control glass is classified into single, double, and triple glazed types, each designed to manage solar heat gain and enhance energy efficiency. Single glazed units consist of a single pane, offering minimal thermal insulation. Double glazed units feature two panes, improving insulation and reducing heat transfer. Triple glazed systems contain three panes, providing superior thermal performance, noise reduction, and enhanced solar control. These types cater to varying architectural needs, balancing aesthetics, energy efficiency, and occupant comfort.</p></p>
