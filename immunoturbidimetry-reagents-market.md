<p><p>The immunoturbidimetry reagents market primarily consists of two types: common immune turbidimetric reagents and latex enhanced immune turbidimetric reagents. Common immune turbidimetric reagents are used in standard assays to measure protein concentrations by detecting antibody-antigen complexes. In contrast, latex enhanced immune turbidimetric reagents incorporate latex particles to improve sensitivity and specificity in the detection process. This enhancement allows for the measurement of lower concentrations of analytes, making it essential for various clinical diagnostics and research applications.</p></p>
