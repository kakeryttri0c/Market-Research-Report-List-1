<p><p>The Emergency Beacon Transmitter market includes Personal Locator Beacons (PLBs), which are compact devices that allow individuals to alert authorities in emergencies. Emergency Position-Indicating Radio Beacons (EPIRBs) are designed for maritime use, enabling boats and ships to signal distress and provide location information. Emergency Locator Transmitters (ELTs) are installed in aircraft to automatically transmit distress signals following a crash. Together, these devices enhance safety and rescue operations across land, sea, and air by ensuring reliable communication during emergencies.</p></p>
