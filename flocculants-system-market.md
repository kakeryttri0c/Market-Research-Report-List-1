Inorganic flocculants, such as alum and ferric chloride, are widely used for cost-effectiveness and efficiency. Synthetic organic flocculants offer high performance and versatility, ideal for industrial applications. Natural flocculants, derived from plant or animal sources, are eco-friendly alternatives gaining traction in wastewater treatment and environmental applications.</p></p>
