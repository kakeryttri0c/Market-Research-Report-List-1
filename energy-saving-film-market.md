<p><p>The energy-saving film market is segmented into three main types: unidirectional film, bidirectional film, and others. Unidirectional film allows solar heat to pass through in one direction while blocking it from the opposite side, enhancing energy efficiency in buildings. Bidirectional film permits two-way heat transfer, offering flexibility for various applications. Other films may include multifunctional options that combine energy savings with additional features like UV protection or glare reduction, catering to different consumer needs and preferences in energy management.</p></p>
