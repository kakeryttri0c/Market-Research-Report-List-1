<p><p>The silicone gum blend market can be categorized into three viscosity types: low, medium, and high viscosity. Low viscosity silicone blends offer flexibility and are ideal for applications requiring ease of processing and spreading, such as sealants and adhesives. Medium viscosity blends balance processing and performance, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including coatings and moldings. High viscosity blends provide thickness and stability, often used in specialized applications like automotive or electrical insulating materials. Each type caters to distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
