<p><p>The brass foils market encompasses various copper types, each catering to specific applications. Oxygen-Free High Conductivity Copper (OFHC) offers exceptional thermal and electrical conductivity, making it ideal for high-performance electronics. Electrolytic Tough Pitch (ETP) Copper is widely used for electrical applications due to its good conductivity and affordability. Deoxidized High Phosphorous (DHP) Copper features enhanced mechanical properties and corrosion resistance, suited for harsh environments, while Deoxidized Low Phosphorous (DLP) Copper provides similar benefits but with less phosphorus content, ensuring reliable performance.</p></p>
