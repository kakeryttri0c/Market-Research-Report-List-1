<p><strong>The Desalting Columns Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spin Desalting Columns</li><li>Gravity Desalting Columns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Desalting columns are used in biochemical applications to remove small molecules and salts from protein solutions. Spin desalting columns utilize centrifugation to facilitate the separation process, ensuring rapid and efficient desalting. In contrast, gravity desalting columns rely on the natural force of gravity, allowing the solution to pass through a porous medium, typically providing a more hands-off, passive method. Both types cater to various laboratory needs, balancing speed and efficiency for protein purification tasks.</p></p>
