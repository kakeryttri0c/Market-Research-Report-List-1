<p><p>The Glass Reactor System market comprises primarily two types: Single Jacketed and Double Jacketed Reactor Systems. Single Jacketed Reactors feature a single outer layer for heating or cooling, making them suitable for simpler processes. In contrast, Double Jacketed Reactors have two layers, providing enhanced thermal management and greater control over temperature fluctuations, which is ideal for sensitive reactions. Both systems cater to various chemical, pharmaceutical, and laboratory applications, emphasizing safety and efficient heat transfer in a controlled environment.</p></p>
