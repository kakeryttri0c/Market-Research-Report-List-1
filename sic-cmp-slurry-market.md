<p><ul><li>Bulk Removal Slurry</li><li>Fine Polish Slurry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SiC CMP slurry market is categorized into two main types: bulk removal slurry and fine polish slurry. Bulk removal slurry is designed for the rapid removal of material during the initial grinding phases, effectively reducing the wafer thickness. In contrast, fine polish slurry is used for the final finishing stage, ensuring a smooth surface and precise surface flatness. Both types are crucial in the chemical mechanical planarization process, catering to different stages of semiconductor manufacturing involving silicon carbide substrates.</p></p>
