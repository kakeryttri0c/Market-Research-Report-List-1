<p><p>A Magnetic Level Switch is a device used to detect the level of liquids in various applications. It operates on the principle of magnetic buoyancy, where a float containing a magnet rises or falls with the liquid level, triggering a switch at predetermined points. These switches are valued for their reliability, simplicity, and minimal maintenance requirements, making them suitable for diverse industries, including water treatment, chemicals, and food and beverage.</p><p>The Magnetic Level Switch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period.
