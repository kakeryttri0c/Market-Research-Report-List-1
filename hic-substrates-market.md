<p><strong>The HIC Substrates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Al2O3 HIC Substrates</li><li>AlN HIC Substrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HIC substrates, or high thermal conductivity substrates, are essential in electronics for efficient heat management. Al2O3 HIC substrates, made from aluminum oxide, offer good thermal conductivity and electrical insulation, making them suitable for various applications, including LED and power electronics. In contrast, AlN HIC substrates, composed of aluminum nitride, provide superior thermal conductivity and are ideal for high-power devices and RF applications. Both types cater to the growing demand for advanced thermal management in electronic components.</p></p>
