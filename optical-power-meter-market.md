<p><p>The optical power meter market encompasses three main types: benchtop meters, portable meters, and virtual meters. Benchtop meters are designed for laboratory use, offering high precision and stability for extensive testing. Portable meters are compact and battery-operated, ideal for field applications where mobility is crucial. Virtual meters utilize software interfaces to measure optical power through connected devices, enabling remote access and data management.
