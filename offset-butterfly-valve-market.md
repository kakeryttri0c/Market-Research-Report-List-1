<p><strong>The Offset Butterfly Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Eccentric Butterfly Valves</li><li>Triple Eccentric Butterfly Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offset butterfly valve market comprises primarily double eccentric and triple eccentric valves, distinguished by their design and functionality. Double eccentric butterfly valves feature an offset disc that reduces friction between the disc and the seat, enhancing sealing at higher pressures. In contrast, triple eccentric butterfly valves incorporate an additional offset, allowing for tighter sealing and resistance to wear, making them ideal for high-temperature and high-pressure applications. Both types are essential in industries requiring efficient flow control and reliable performance.</p></p>
