<p><ul><li>Time-resolved Immunofluorescence</li><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The testosterone test kit market features various testing types, including Time-resolved Immunofluorescence (TRIF) and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA). TRIF utilizes time-resolved detection to enhance sensitivity and specificity for measuring testosterone levels in samples, making it suitable for precise diagnostics. In contrast, CLIA employs chemiluminescent reactions to produce light, which correlates with hormone concentrations, allowing for rapid and high-throughput testing. Both methods are essential for accurately diagnosing hormonal imbalances and monitoring testosterone therapy in clinical settings.</p></p>
